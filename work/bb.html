Work | BADAN BAHASA

The challenge

Digitizing rare books of the language agency ministry education and culture.

In 2020, since the Covid-19 virus was declared a global pandemic, I am grateful to get a freelancer job to carry out a project to digitize rare books owned by the language agency library. This job is very risky for the book to be damaged because the book is old and the paper and cover are easily broken.

The result

With the project of digitizing rare books in the language body library, it is easier for students and the public to learn a lot about the history of the Indonesian language and the Indonesian state. Because these rare books discuss a lot about the language and history of the formation of the Indonesian state, the publication of this book covers the years of independence in 1945, even the oldest was in 1880.
